About Safety Curtain

Quick customisable text banner for streaming and presentations

Safety Curtain is a free welcome slide or static text banner that you can use for lots of different things like:

  • video conferencing
  • greeting before you start a talk or presentation
  • pre-broadcast slide for YouTube and Twitch
  • Leaving nice messages on your coworkers' computer :-)

Just set your own text using the Customise link, and then go full screen to show a nice banner while you're waiting to start your call or presentation.
